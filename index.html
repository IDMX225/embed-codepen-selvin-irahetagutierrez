Pen: CSS 3D Solar System

by Julian Garnier

an interactive 3D simulation of our solar system. It allows users to explore planets, moons, and other celestial bodies in a visually engaging way. Users can zoom in on planets, learn about their features, and even view their orbits. It's a great educational tool for anyone interested in astronomy, making it easier to understand the scale and dynamics of our solar system.
